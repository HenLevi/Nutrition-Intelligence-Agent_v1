Balanced Daily Nutrition

A balanced diet includes all macronutrients:
- Carbohydrates
- Proteins
- Fats

General daily structure:
- Regular meals spaced throughout the day
- Combination of protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats at each meal

Healthy fat sources:
- Olive oil
- Avocado
- Nuts and seeds

Carbohydrates provide energy for daily activities and exercise.
Whole-food sources are preferred for long-term health.
